JH Chamber Annual Report

Typography/Infographics

*This project was completed while working for Creative Curiosity*

I was tasked in designing the 2021-22 annual report for Jackson Hole Chamber of Commerce. The goal of the report was to inform the viewer of the achievements and profits the company made over the year. Taking inspiration from the report the year prior, I went through and updated all the information and redesigned some of the pages to better fit the new information. I created unique pie charts, graphs, and maps, intending to offer the audience a clearer grasp of the information at hand. These customized visual aids were designed to enhance comprehension and provide a more accessible way for viewers to absorb and comprehend the data presented.
