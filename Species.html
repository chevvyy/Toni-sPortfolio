Endangered Species Infographic

Infographics

I worked on creating visual graphics to raise awareness about endangered animals. To grab attention, I used soft, muted colors, highlighting crucial details in red. To keep things interesting, I designed unique graphs, aiming to prevent monotony and make the information stick in people's minds. There are various causes behind animals facing extinction, many of which are because of human actions. I believe that by spreading awareness, we can make a difference. The more people know about this situation, the more they can help. It's important to showcase this issue in a way that captures attention and encourages action, and that's what I aimed to do through these visual graphics.
