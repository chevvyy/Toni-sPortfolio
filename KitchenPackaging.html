Package Design

For this personal project, I dove into the world of packaging. I chose some products from Thyme and Table to start my redesigning. My aim was to reimagine the package design, breaking away from the conventional styles found in stores. Beginning with extensive research to unearth fresh inspiration, I then delved into thumbnail sketches, refining and shaping the package's aesthetic. Transitioning to digital tools, I envisioned the packaging as a standout presence on shelves. Employing black as the primary color, I sought to create a visual impact.
