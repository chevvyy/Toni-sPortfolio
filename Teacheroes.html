CFC Techeroes

Advertising

*This project was completed while working for Creative Curiosity*

Champions for Children, in Jackson Hole WY, began featuring different teachers during each month of the year. The goal was to raise awareness of the amazing teachers in Jackson and to help them out with donation and volunteering. I was in charge of designing the ads for each teacher. Once I cut the person out of their pictures, I could turn them into a hero. The ads included were physical flyers, social media posts and gifs.
