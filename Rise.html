Rise of AI Infographics

Infographics

AI is getting really big in our lives. Some folks are worried it might take their jobs because it's so new and we don't know a lot about it. I decided to look into AI to see if we should worry or if there's something good about it. I did research to find out more. Then, I made a fun way to learn about AI. I used pictures and made a special website that's easy to use. It's like a sneak peek into the future where you can play around and learn all about AI in a cool way.
