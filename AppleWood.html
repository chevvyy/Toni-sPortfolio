AppleWood Bistro

Branding Design/Typography

I designed a brand for a BBQ restaurant for a personal project. I looked into who would come to eat there and how the place would look and feel. It's a mix of modern and a bit of old-fashioned charm. For the logo, I made it look like a branding iron. I used simple colors like red and black, adding some wood touches. The bistro has a theme that's both modern and a bit old-fashioned, meant for everyone, young and old. It's like a blend of the latest style with a touch of the countryside, making it a cozy place for anyone who loves good BBQ.
